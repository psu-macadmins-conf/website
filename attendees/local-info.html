Welcome to Happy Valley!

Whether you're a first-time visitor or a returning guest, we want to make your trip to the MacAdmins Conference as smooth and enjoyable as possible. Below you'll find helpful information about what to do, where to go, and what to expect while you're here at Penn State.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Things for Kids & Families

Bringing family along? Central Pennsylvania offers some great attractions that are fun for all ages:

  • Penn’s Cave & Wildlife Park: America’s only all-water cavern and wildlife park — enjoy a guided boat tour through limestone caves and explore the outdoor animal park.
  • DelGrosso’s Park & Laguna Splash: A family-friendly amusement and water park located about 45 minutes from campus. Great rides, slides, and famous Italian food.

🧴 Practical Info

  • Pharmacies: Several options nearby, including CVS (Hills Plaza) and Rite Aid (Atherton Street).
  • Urgent Care: Mount Nittany ExpressCARE or MedExpress are local walk-in clinics.
  • EV Charging: Car charging stations are available at the East and West Parking Decks, as well as select downtown locations. See Penn State Transportation for details.

🌤 What’s the Weather Like in July?

July in State College is warm and often humid. Expect:

  • Average Highs: 82–86°F (28–30°C)
  • Evening Lows: 60–65°F (15–18°C)
  • Bring sunscreen, sunglasses — and pack an umbrella just in case!
